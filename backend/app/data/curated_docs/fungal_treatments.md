# Fungal Disease Treatments

Fungal diseases like early blight, late blight, rust, and powdery mildew are common in crops like tomatoes, potatoes, and apples.

## General Treatment
- **Fungicides**: Apply copper-based fungicides or chlorothalonil.
- **Cultural Practices**: Ensure proper spacing for airflow, avoid overhead watering, and remove infected leaves immediately.
- **Crop Rotation**: Do not plant the same family of crops in the same spot for at least 3 years.
